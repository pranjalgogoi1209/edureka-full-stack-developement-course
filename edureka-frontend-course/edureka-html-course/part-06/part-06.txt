# NEW TAG IN HTML 5 =>

    i. header: this tag is always used in top of our pages or around headings basically.
    ii. footer: this tag is used at the bottom of a page to add additional info.
    iii. article: this tag is used to have article like layout.
    iv. section: this is used to divide our page into different section.
    v. video and audio tags: these tags are used to add audio and video on our page.
